Introduction

The U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs Whole Health approach to care includes a Circle of Health and Personal Health Inventory along with extensive guidance on how to implement these practices for Veterans.

This FHIR Implementation Guide (IG) is derived from the HL7 Person-Centered Outcomes (PCO) FHIR IG specification.
